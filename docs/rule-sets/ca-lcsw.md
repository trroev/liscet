# California — LCSW CE Rule Set

**Status:** 🟡 Pending partner confirmation · **Version:** 1 · **Researched:** 2026-06-08

Governing board: California Board of Behavioral Sciences (BBS).

## Renewal cycle

`renewalCycleMonths: 24` — biennial. The period spans the two years from the day
after the prior expiration date through the current expiration date.

## Total hours

`totalHours: 36` — completed each renewal period.

## Category minimums

| Category (`SubjectCategory`) | Min hours | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `law-and-ethics` | 6 | Required every renewal period. |

## Format constraints

None. (BBS does not cap online/home-study hours for LCSW renewal.)

`formatConstraints: []`

## Special requirements

| Category | Min hours | Recurrence | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `suicide-risk` | 6 | `one-time` | Coursework or supervised applied experience in suicide risk assessment and intervention. Required for licensees who renew/reactivate after 2021-01-01, unless proof was submitted at licensure. |
| `telehealth` | 3 | `one-time` | Provision of mental-health services via telehealth, including telehealth law and ethics. Required for licensees who renew/reactivate after 2023-07-01. |

## Carry-over

No carry-over of excess hours into the next period.

`carryOverMaxHours: null`

## Record retention (informational, not evaluated)

Retain CE certificates/transcripts for at least **2 years** after the renewal
period in which they were earned. Do not submit certificates with renewal.
