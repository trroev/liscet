# Connecticut — LICSW CE Rule Set

**Status:** 🟡 Pending partner confirmation · **Version:** 1 · **Researched:** 2026-06-08

Governing board: Connecticut Department of Public Health (DPH), Practitioner
Licensing.

## Renewal cycle

`renewalCycleMonths: 12` — annual registration period.

## Total hours

`totalHours: 15` — completed each registration period.

## Category minimums

| Category (`SubjectCategory`) | Min hours | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `cultural-competency` | 1 | Each registration period. |

## Format constraints

| Kind | Formats | Value | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `max-hours` | `home-study` | 10 | At most 10 of the 15 hours may be online/home-study, so at least 5 must be in-person or synchronous online. |

## Special requirements

| Category | Min hours | Recurrence | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `veterans-mental-health` | 2 | `{ everyMonths: 72 }` | Mental-health conditions common to veterans and their families. Required for the first renewal period after licensure, then once every 6 years. |

## Carry-over

No carry-over of excess hours into the next period.

`carryOverMaxHours: null`

## Record retention (informational, not evaluated)

Retain a certificate of completion for at least **3 years** following the renewal
date the activity satisfies.
